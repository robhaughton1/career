Respiratory Therapist

Respiratory Therapy is a medical branch that dives into nursing a patient with respiratory (lung) issues. On the job, you would typically be caring for a patient, regularly checking their vitals, and giving emotional support.

Skills, Abilities, and Knowledge

A typical RT would be knowledgable on the human body, knowing how to read vital signs, how to check pulses, and so forth. The typical skills of an RT would be being patient, caring, and nonjudgemental.

Skills and abilities would pertain to what the RT learned with the medical information they learned. They typically would know the skils I listed above.

What is their Personality?

A good RT is patient, empathetic, and calm in most dire situations so that they can bring out the best in their patients well being. Technology for a RT can be CPAP machines, respiratory machines, IV, or anything pertaining to the thoraxic region of the body. Their average salary is 70,000 a year, but it can reach a wopping 100,000. Education wise, they typically need an associate or bachelors degree.

Why should someone go into this role? Maybe not RT specificially, but those who want to make a difference in someones life can definitely find peace in the medical field. It varies from person to person, but their is always a branch that suits a person the most.
